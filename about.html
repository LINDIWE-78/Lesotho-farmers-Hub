About Lesotho Hub

Connecting Farmers and Customers Across Lesotho

Our Mission

Lesotho Hub is an online platform created to connect farmers with customers across Lesotho. Our mission is to promote local agriculture by giving farmers a digital space where they can advertise their products.

  • Promote local agriculture and small businesses
  • Strengthen the economy and reduce poverty
  • Improve food security in Lesotho
  • Empower farmers with modern tools
