Business Tips for Farmers

Keep your products fresh and well-packaged.

Advertise on social media to reach more customers.

Set fair and competitive prices.

Offer discounts for bulk purchases.

Work with other farmers for cooperative sales.

Use modern tools to track production and sales.

Always communicate politely with customers.

Save part of your income for reinvestment.
